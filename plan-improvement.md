## Plan Improvement {#plan-improvement}
We offer three plans: Free, Basic, and Pro. The Free tier includes core features with a 5-project limit, plus priority support and a 1-hour onboarding session. The Basic plan adds advanced features, including automated workflows and a 10-project limit, while the Pro plan includes all Basic features, plus unlimited projects, dedicated support, and a personalized onboarding experience.
